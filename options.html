🗂️ Archive Browser

Settings & Configuration

📋 About This Extension

Archive Browser helps you safely browse web archive sites with Flash support via Ruffle 0.3.0 and prevents unwanted redirects.

General Settings

Prevents automatic redirects when clicking links on archive sites. Links open in new tabs instead.

Automatically loads Ruffle Flash emulator to play legacy Flash content on archived pages.

Blocks analytics and tracking pixels from Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and similar services.

Archive Site Preferences

Performance

Preloads the Ruffle player for faster Flash content loading.

Caches downloaded Flash files for faster subsequent access.

Advanced

Enables verbose logging in browser console for troubleshooting.
